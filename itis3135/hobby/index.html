Best Season to Go

Depending on where you decide to go, the seasons matter! Below is a table of seasons and places that will give you the best experience for your trips!


Places Best Season Why
Chimney Rock, NC Late Fall The crispy and cold fall air during this time allows the air to become dense. This forms fog and moist air giving you a cloud-like experience at the top of Chimney Rock!
Tacoma, WA Late Winter to Early Spring Moving more towards early spring, if you deicde to go to Washington state during this season, you'll be able to experience at least two different seasons! The drive will look like spring, but once you get to the mountains, it immediately becomes winter again.
Oak Island, NC Early Fall As many of you many know, early fall in the southeastern United States is hurricane season. While it is so, this is definitely the best time of year to go to the beach. It's not too hot and not too cold!

Places to Go

If you enjoy the thrill of going off-road while traveling, two of the best places to visit is Uwharrie National Forrest and Old NC-105 in North Carolina. These trails are one of the most adventurous trails I've ever been to. You get to challenge your 4x4 vehicle to the ultimate test!

If you are a beginner to going off-road, two of the easiest trails in Uwharrie are the Woodrun Trail and Wolf Den. Try these trails out and see if off-roading is a fit for you!

Preparing for the Trip

When planning for a trip, it is important to pack the essentials as well as plan for unexpected emergencies! Here are some things you may want to consider:

  • Gas: Tracking the mileage before hand is important. It allows you to calculate how much gas you may need and where you'll need to stop!
  • Snack: Packing enough snacks for the trip is a must! Especially coffee and water!
  • Tools & Gear: These are important to have anywhere! New cars can also break down. If you've got a handy person with you and some tools, you'll be back on the road in no time!
  • Passengers: There is no great trip without those you spend it with!
