ABOUT MACH ATLUS AVIATION

Welcome to Mach Atlus Aviation, where expertise meets precision in aircraft maintenance, inspection, cleaning, and repair. With over 14 years of dedicated experience, Mach Atlus Aviation is a one-person company committed to ensuring the optimal performance and safety of a diverse range of aircraft, from a variety of military fighter jets to private and commercial fleets.



About the Owner

The driver behind Mach Atlus Aviation is our founder, Eric Samsombath. With a versatile background in various areas of aircraft maintenance spanning over 14 years, Eric brings a wealth of hands-on experience and in-depth knowledge to the industry. Having served in the military and worked on high-performance aircraft, Eric understands the critical importance of precision and reliability in aviation.

Eric is a certified Aircraft and Powerplant (A&P) mechanic, holding a license issued by the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA). This certification signifies Eric's expertise in maintaining and repairing aircrafts, meeting the rigorous standards set by the FAA. Currently, Eric is working towards obtaining his Inspection Authorization (IA) license, further expanding his qualifications and ensuring Mach Atlus Aviation remains up-to-date of aviation excellence and expectations.
