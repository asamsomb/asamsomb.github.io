Website Evaluations

Ling's Cars 🔗

This website offers a variety of vehicles which users can browse and compare prices with. The website offers a Car Leasing online service with a given response time.

  • Contrast: The website is very heavy with its colors and use of images and gifs.
  • Repetition: The lack of repetition within each clickable link becomes very distracting when trying to find specific information.
  • Alignment: The alignment of the website does not offer distinguishable sections.
  • Proximity: The proximity of this website layout is too close...literally. While it is easy to navigate witht the left-hand navigation menu, the descriptions for the products do not align appropriately together.

Target 🔗

This website is a major retailer website that offers their users a more interactive experience. From online shopping to picking up in-store, users are able navigate easily to a variety of products and services offered by the company.

  • Contrast: This company's website shows great detail in their colors within their layouts by integrating seasonal-like colors and products.
  • Repetition: The repetition of the overall website is distinguishable and offers users ease in finding the prices and descriptions of an item.
  • Alignment: The alignment of the website is kept constant and overall does not seem distracting when clicking various links.
  • Proximity: The proximity of the products show coordination and easy navigation. Items listed in different categories are identical in proportion.
